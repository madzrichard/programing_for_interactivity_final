A New York City based campaign to combat harrassment on public transit

What is this campaign?

The Other Public Zoo is a New York City based advertising campaign that looks to lessen the gender inequality felt within public transit environments. With public transit so essential & almost unavoidable in NYC, we thought it was the perfect place. A place to get men to recognize that women can feel preyed upon while also looking to prove to women they don't always have to feel this way. But for now it's a zoo out there.

And why is it needed?

We found that within the environment of public transit, many woman feel as if they don’t have the ability to move from origin to destination without worrying about making a “wrong choice", causing an unsafe or uncomfortable situation. This constant worry can make women feel trapped, a problem in itself. With that we also need to address men's neglect or indifference to the injustices that are usually directed soley towards women in these environemnets.
